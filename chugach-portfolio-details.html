Chugach Government Solutions Chooses Oracle's HCM Software

After conducting an assessment of its existing HCM systems to identify functional gaps, Chugach Government Solutions went to bid for a new HCM vendor. Rightfully so, Chugach choosed Oracle Fusion HCM system after Oracle met their key business objectives and key business requirements. As sole Principal Proposal Consultant, we were able to assist Oracle in its RFP efforts to best market its HCM expertise and capabilities that Chugach was looking for in its next iteration of HCM systems.
