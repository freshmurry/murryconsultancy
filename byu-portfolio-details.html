BYU Idaho Chooses NetSuite as ERP Software Vendor

After conducting an assessment of its existing ERP systems to identify functional gaps, BYU went to bid for a new ERP vendor for its campus bookstore back office operations. Rightfully so, BYU choosed Oracle NetSuite's ERP system after NetSuite met their key business objectives and key business requirements. As sole Principal Proposal Consultant, we were able to help NetSuite in its RFP efforts to best market its ERP expertise and capabilities that BYU was looking for in its next iteration of ERP systems.
