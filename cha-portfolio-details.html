The Chicago Housing Authority Chooses Oracle's Human Capital Management Cloud (HCM Cloud)_

After conducting an assessment of its existing HCM systems to identify functional gaps, The Chicago Housing Authority went to bid for a new HCM vendor. Rightfully so, CHA choosed Oracle Fusion HCM system after Oracle met their key business objectives and key business requirements. As sole Principal Proposal Consultant, we were able to assist Oracle in its RFP efforts to best market its HCM expertise and capabilities that The Chicago Housing Authority was looking for in its next iteration of HCM systems.
